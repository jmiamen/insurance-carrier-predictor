Golden Eagle Final Expense Whole Life Insurance

Product Summary:
The Golden Eagle Final Expense plan is designed specifically for seniors age 50-85 who need affordable whole life coverage for burial expenses and final expenses.

Key Features:
- No medical exam
- Guaranteed acceptance options available
- Multiple underwriting tiers (Premier, Plus, Standard, Graded, Modified, Guaranteed Issue)
- Level premiums
- Immediate death benefit on Premier and Plus tiers

Coverage Specifications:
- Issue Ages: 50-85
- Face Amounts: $2,500 - $35,000
- Premium Modes: Monthly, Quarterly, Annual
- Policy Type: Whole Life

State Availability:
Texas, Colorado, Michigan, Missouri, Kansas, Oklahoma, Nebraska, Arkansas, Louisiana

Underwriting Tiers:

Premier Tier:
- Healthiest applicants
- Full death benefit from day one
- Best premium rates

Plus Tier:
- Good health, some conditions accepted
- Full death benefit from day one

Standard Tier:
- Moderate health conditions
- Full benefit day one

Graded Tier:
- More serious health conditions
- Graded death benefit (first 2 years)

Guaranteed Issue:
- No health questions
- Modified benefit period

Health Conditions Accepted (varies by tier):
- Diabetes (Type 1 and Type 2)
- Peripheral neuropathy
- COPD
- Asthma
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- History of cancer (depending on type and time since treatment)
- Heart disease (stable)

Smokers and tobacco users accepted with appropriate rate class.

The Golden Eagle policy provides peace of mind for families by ensuring funeral and final expenses are covered.
