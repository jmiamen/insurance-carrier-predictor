===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Golden Eagle is ELCO Mutual's traditional whole life insurance product offering permanent lifetime coverage with flexible premium payment options. Policyholders can choose Life Pay (payments through age 100) or Limited Pay options (10 year 20 year 30 year or paid up at age 65). All variants provide guaranteed level premiums that never increase guaranteed death benefit and guaranteed cash value accumulation. As a participating policy eligible for annual dividends from mutual company. Includes accelerated death benefit rider at no cost covering terminal illness nursing home confinement home health care and chronic illness. Simplified underwriting with typically no medical exams. Optional riders include waiver of premium accidental death benefit and single premium life. Maximum face amount 300000. Issue ages vary by payment plan from 0 to 85. Ideal for final expense planning estate building family protection and leaving a legacy. Backed by ELCO Mutual policyholder owned company in business since 1946.
