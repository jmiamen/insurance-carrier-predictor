===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Platinum Eagle is ELCO Mutual's single premium whole life insurance product designed to provide permanent coverage with a one time lump sum payment. This payment immediately generates a guaranteed death benefit and cash value with no additional payments ever needed. The policy remains in force for life or until age 121. Ideal for estate planning wealth transfer and gift giving especially to children and grandchildren. As a participating policy it is eligible for annual dividends that can increase cash value and death benefit. Includes accelerated death benefit rider at no cost covering terminal illness nursing home confinement home health care and chronic illness. Simplified underwriting with typically no medical exams required. Available for ages 0 to 85 with maximum net amount at risk of 300000. Backed by mutual company owned by policyholders in business since 1946.
