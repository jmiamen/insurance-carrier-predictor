  STATE: Must reside in approved state
  HEALTH: Must meet simplified issue health questions
  FACE_AMOUNT: Must be within allowable range

APPROVAL_FACTORS:
  - Answers to health questions on application
  - Prescription drug history review
  - PHI results if required
  - MVR results if ordered
  - Paramedical exam results if required
  - Rate class assignment based on health tobacco use and build

DECLINE_FACTORS:
  - Recent cancer diagnosis or active treatment within 24 months
  - Recent heart attack or stroke within 24 months
  - Uncontrolled diabetes with complications
  - Oxygen use for lung condition
  - AIDS or HIV with complications
  - Cirrhosis of liver
  - End stage renal disease on dialysis
  - Pending surgery or tests for serious condition
  - Decline on other life insurance within last 12 months
  - Hazardous occupation or avocation

PREFERRED_PLUS_QUALIFICATION:
  - No tobacco use 60 months
  - Excellent health no medications except vitamins
  - Perfect build within preferred plus chart
  - No family history of heart disease or cancer before age 60
  - No aviation or hazardous activities
  - Available Simple Term 20 DLX only
