MUTUAL OF OMAHA LIFE INSURANCE COMPANY

Living Promise Whole Life Insurance

Product Overview:
Living Promise is a simplified issue whole life insurance policy designed for individuals aged 45-85. This permanent life insurance provides guaranteed death benefit protection with level premiums that never increase.

Coverage Details:
- Issue Ages: 45-85
- Face Amounts: $2,000 - $50,000
- Premium: Level, never increases
- Death Benefit: Guaranteed, level benefit from day one
- Cash Value: Builds over time

State Availability:
Available in Texas, Florida, Georgia, California, New York, Illinois, Ohio, Pennsylvania, North Carolina, Virginia, Arizona, Tennessee, Indiana, Missouri, and Wisconsin.

Underwriting:
Simplified issue - no medical exam required. Health questions are asked on the application.

Health Conditions Accepted:
- Controlled diabetes (Type 2)
- High blood pressure (controlled with medication)
- High cholesterol (controlled)
- History of heart disease (stable, no recent events)

The Living Promise policy includes:
- Terminal Illness Accelerated Benefit Rider
- Nursing Home Accelerated Benefit Rider
- Guaranteed insurability

Smoker/Tobacco Users: Accepted with appropriate premium adjustments.

Perfect for:
- Final expense planning
- Mortgage protection
- Leaving a legacy
- Supplementing existing coverage
