===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Presidio Plus with Irrevocable Funeral Trust is ELCO Mutual's guaranteed issue single premium whole life insurance designed specifically for funeral planning with asset protection benefits. Face amounts from 2500 to 15000 cover comprehensive funeral expenses. Everyone qualifies with no medical exams or health questions. The key feature is ELCO's Irrevocable Funeral Trust (IFT) available at no additional cost to policyholders. Once the policy is irrevocably assigned to the IFT it may become an exempt asset protected from creditors nursing homes and government assistance programs potentially helping qualify for Medicaid. Funds are paid directly to the designated funeral home within approximately 48 hours avoiding probate delays. Estate is contingent beneficiary. Covers all funeral goods and services including basic services embalming facilities casket vault cemetery charges transportation flowers and more. Ideal for seniors concerned about nursing home costs depleting assets wanting to secure funeral funding protect assets and qualify for Medicaid. Eligibility for Medicaid not guaranteed consult Elder Law Attorney. Policy form ICC21 TSPL 21 may vary by state.
