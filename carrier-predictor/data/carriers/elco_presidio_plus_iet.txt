===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Presidio Plus with Irrevocable Estate Trust is ELCO Mutual's guaranteed issue single premium whole life insurance designed for comprehensive estate planning and wealth transfer. Face amounts from 15000 to 100000 cover funeral expenses immediate outstanding costs and provide liquidity to family during estate settlement. Ages 0 to 99 qualify with no medical exams or health questions. The key feature is ELCO's Irrevocable Estate Trust (IET) available at no additional cost. Policy is assigned to IET to protect and fulfill intentions. At death company pays funeral home when instructed for expenses then delivers remaining benefits to beneficiaries within 48 hours. Avoids probate ensuring quick access. Death benefit is income tax free enabling seamless wealth transfer. Solves the problem of assets being tied up in probate for weeks or months by providing immediate liquidity. Customizable solution planned with licensed professional to determine appropriate coverage for final expenses outstanding obligations and family financial needs during settlement. Ideal for those wanting comprehensive estate planning guaranteed acceptance and income tax advantaged wealth transfer. Policy form ICC21 TSPL 21 may vary by state. Backed by ELCO Mutual policyholder owned company in business over 75 years.
