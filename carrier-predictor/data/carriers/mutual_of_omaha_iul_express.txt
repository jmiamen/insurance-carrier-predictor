===OPTIONAL_RIDERS===
RIDER: Accidental_Death_Benefit
  COST: Additional premium
  BENEFIT: Additional death benefit if death results from accident
  MAXIMUM: Equal to base policy face amount
  TERMINATION_AGE: 70
  MAXIMUM_COMBINED_LIMIT_AGES_18_TO_25: 100000
  MAXIMUM_COMBINED_LIMIT_AGES_26_TO_60: 250000

RIDER: Disability_Benefits_Rider
  REQUIRES: Disability Supplemental Application
  COST: Additional premium
  BENEFIT: Waives premium if insured becomes totally disabled
  QUALIFICATION: Total disability before age 60 lasting 6 consecutive months
  DURATION: Waiver continues while disabled before age 65
  BUILD_CHART: Separate DI Rider Maximum Weight column applies

RIDER: Children's_Rider
  REQUIRES: Children's Rider Supplemental Application
  COST: Additional premium
  BENEFIT: Term insurance on children
  ELIGIBLE_CHILDREN: Age 15 days to 18 years
  FACE_AMOUNTS: Varies by product
  CONVERSION: Can convert to permanent policy at age 25 without evidence of insurability
  MAXIMUM_COMBINED_LIMIT: 10000 per child all products
