===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Silver Eagle is ELCO Mutual's final expense whole life insurance product designed to help individuals ages 50 to 85 prepare for end of life costs and protect their families from financial burdens. Offers four coverage options immediate coverage (Premier Plus Standard classes) with full benefit day one for healthier applicants graded benefit (30 percent year 1 70 percent year 2 full benefit year 3) for those with some health issues modified benefit (return of premium plus interest years 1 to 2 full benefit year 3) and guaranteed issue (no health questions guaranteed acceptance return of premium plus interest years 1 to 2 full benefit year 3). All classes provide guaranteed level premiums and cash value. Immediate graded and modified classes are participating and eligible for dividends. No medical exams or tests ever required. Instant decisions. Face amounts from 2500 to 35000. Single premium life rider available on immediate coverage policies. Ideal for funeral cost coverage debt payoff and leaving a legacy. Backed by ELCO Mutual policyholder owned mutual company in business since 1946.
