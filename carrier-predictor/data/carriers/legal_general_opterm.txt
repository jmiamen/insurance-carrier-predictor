===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
OPTerm is Legal & General America's level premium term life insurance with a decreasing death benefit structure. Available through Banner Life Insurance Company in 49 states and DC and William Penn Life Insurance Company exclusively in New York. Offers 7 term length options 10 15 20 25 30 35 and 40 years with market leading guaranteed level premiums during the initial term period. Minimum face amount 100000. Issue ages 20 to 75 depending on term length and tobacco status with high maximum issue ages as competitive advantage. After the initial level term period death benefits decrease and premiums change providing extended coverage. Renewable and convertible for level premium period or up to attained age 70 whichever first. Policies issued age 66 plus convertible during first 5 years. Multiple risk classifications including Standard Plus Nontobacco applied to most rated cases. Family cancer history does not prevent preferred class consideration. Accelerated death benefit automatically included. Optional riders include waiver of premium term riders 10 15 20 and childrens rider not available NY or MD. Annual policy fees 90 Banner Life 80 William Penn. Backed by worldwide Legal & General Group. Ideal for temporary coverage needs income replacement mortgage protection and older applicants seeking term insurance with conversion options.
