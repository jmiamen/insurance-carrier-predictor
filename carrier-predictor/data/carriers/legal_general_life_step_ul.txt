===PRODUCT_DESCRIPTION===
Life Step UL is Legal & General America's flexible premium universal life insurance focused on providing death benefit guarantees. Available through Banner Life Insurance Company in 49 states and DC and William Penn Life Insurance Company exclusively in New York. Minimum face amount 50000 designed specifically for term conversions or new sales with lower face amounts. Serves as exclusive conversion product for A List Term policies. Issue ages 20 to 85 all classes. Key feature is coverage guarantee to maturity at age 121 when appropriate premium paid on time according to policy terms. On time premium payment guarantees coverage and policy will not lapse if requirement met. Exception policy loan exceeding cash surrender value results in lapse. Flexible premium structure within policy guidelines. Cash value accumulation with policy loans available. Partial surrenders allowed in approved states. Surrender charges decrease over 14 years. Multiple risk classifications including Standard Plus Nontobacco applied to most rated cases. Family cancer history does not prevent preferred class consideration. Accelerated death benefit automatically included lesser of 75 percent or 500000 for life expectancy 12 months or less. William Penn plans mature at age 121 cannot be extended. Two year contestability and suicide provisions. Premium rates vary by underwriting classification and coverage amount. Backed by worldwide Legal & General Group. Ideal for term conversions permanent coverage with death benefit guarantee focus flexible premium needs and long term planning to age 121.
